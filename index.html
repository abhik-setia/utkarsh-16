ABOUT US

Utkarsh,the largest Hindi cultral fest of North India is one with a motive. Supported by MHRD and organised by ANAMIKA HINDI SOCIETY, the festival was started to promote the importance of Hindi language and with 10 extravagant celebrations,UTKARSH turns 11 this year. Every year the fest has grown and nurtued from plethora of memories, countless dreams and millions of screams. So be a part of something bigger this year, treasure and cherish the aura of joyous moments and witness the colorful blend of culture, drama, music, dance and passion.

Mr. & Ms. Utkarsh

With a touch of glamour and passion, lads and ladies from all the colleges compete to show their talents for the prestigious title of “ Mr. and Ms. Utkarsh”.
First round : This is a written round in which the personality of the participants is tested.
Second round: This is a talent round in which participants are selected on the basis of their talent.
Final round : This round consists of some more sub rounds like fashion parade, audio visual round, performance round and questioning round.

Solo Singing

It is a musical event including all styles of music.

->It is a solo performance.
->Participants are allowed to use any kind of musical instrument, karaoke for best performance.

Duet Singing

It is a musical duet performance. Any kind of Indian Language Movie songs is allowed.

->2 participants make a team (Male-Male, Female-Female, & Male-Female).
->Participants can take help of Musical Instrument Accompanists or Karaoke for Best Performance.

DJ Night
